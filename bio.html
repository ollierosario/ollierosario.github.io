I'm Ollie Rosario! I am an MFA candidate in Digital + Media at the Rhode Island School of Design. I'm interested in multimedia arts, particularly sonic art, and their role in the social landscape in a rapidly evolving world.

Originally from Philadelphia, I moved to Vancouver in 2015 to study at UBC. While there, I gained music programming skills and composed pieces with my peers in the Laptop Orchesta. When not composing, I like to sketch and practice guitar.

It is my hope that as I continue my studies, I can develop my skills and creative practice further.
